YallaPunk 2018 is coming.

YallaPunk was created as a direct response to negative depictions of middle eastern and north african populations. The event is meant to celebrate the creative accomplishments of MENA individuals and serve as a safe, intersectional and inclusive space free from free from sexism, islamophobia, transphobia, homophobia, and bigotry for discourse on social issues. As a community, we will celebarate who we are through music, art, film, poetry, and comedy.

YallaPunk is a project of CultureTrust of Greater Philadelphia
